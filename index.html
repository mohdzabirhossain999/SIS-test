Inspection certificate for exports of brand new boiler and used / second-hand capital machinery to Bangladesh for customs clearance

This certificate is mandatory for customs clearance of brand-new or second-hand machinery into Bangladesh.

When exporting brand new boiler & second-hand capital machinery to Bangladesh, you may be required to obtain an Inspection Certificate, prior to shipment for Letter of Credit negotiation. If your shipments are subject to this clause Search Inspection Services Ltd. (SIS) are approved by the ISOs as per import policy Import Policy 2015-2018 with order by NBR nothi no.(136) sulka muli: / PST audit agency moj/2001 (part-1)/189 dated 02/07/2013 & nothi.no.171) su: ni: o bha: /98/519 dated 03/11/2016 of NBR(endorsed) Government of the People's Republic of Bangladesh.

The inspection certificate is done in order to check quality, valuation, packing and marking against a purchase order or proforma invoice and related letter of credit.

Obtaining an inspection certificate for exporters to Bangladesh is mandatory to avoid delays in clearance upon arrival in Bangladesh and be subject to post landing inspection and certification requiring approval by the Ministry of Commerce. The items will not be able to clear the goods without these certificates as stated in the Import Policy 2015-2018 with order by NBR nothi no.(136) sulka muli: /PST audit agency moj/2001 (part-1)/189 dated 02/07/2013 & nothi.no.171) su: ni: o bha: /98/519 dated 03/11/2016 of NBR (enclosed).

Items for Mandatory Inspection and Certificate Include:

Products Certification
Boiler Quality Certificate
Prime Mover, Dump Truck, Dumper, Mixer Lorry, Self-loader, Hydraulic Crane 10 Years Longevity Certificate
Old or reconditioned Gear box, Bus, Truck, Car or Microbus engines 7 Years Longevity Certificate
Second hand motor vehicle / motor cycle Age Certificate
Used Motor Vehicles Spare Parts Quality Certificate
Chemical Fertilizer Certificate confirming the specification of the fertilizer as per Ministry of Agriculture
MS Billet Prime Quality Certificate

How to Obtain the Inspection Certificate

  • The inspection certificate can be requested by either the exporter or the importer. SIS coordinates the inspection through its global network based on the location of the shipment.
  • After successful inspection and verification of relevant documents, a certificate will be issued and delivered to exporter or importer as agreed.
